Bio

HI! I am Siqiang Luo (骆思强 in Chinese). I am currently a postdoc fellow at Harvard University, with Prof. Stratos Idreos. Before that, I got by Ph.D. degree in computer science at University of Hong Kong, co-supervised by Prof. Ben Kao and Prof. Reynold Cheng. I obtained my bachelor's and master's degrees in computer science at Fudan University.

Research interest

I am broadly interested in data management areas, in particular in adaptive optimization techniques, distributed computation, and efficient graph indexes.
